HOW TO USE?

Open tab viewer

  • Windows: "ALT+T" on focused webpage.
  • Mac: "ALT(OPTION)+T" on focused webpage.

FEATURES

Counting tabs

  • Tabs counting on extension icon

Search

  • Put tab keyword in search bar to find tabs

Navigate page

  • Use "LEFT or RIGHT" in search bar or mouse click on page bar

Navigate tab

  • Use "TAB" in tab lists

Jump to tab

  • Hit "ENTER" on selected tab or use mouse to click tab item

Remove tab

  • Hit 'D' to remove selected tab or use mouse to click the close symbol
